Is my enterprise data secure?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 military-grade encryption. Your data is isolated in your own tenant and is never used to train our base public models. You maintain 100% ownership.

How long does API integration take?

Our REST and GraphQL APIs are designed for seamless integration. Most development teams can connect and start making AI calls within 2 to 4 hours. Comprehensive SDKs are available for Python, Node.js, and Go.

Do you offer custom model fine-tuning?

Yes! On our Pro and Enterprise plans, you can fine-tune our foundation models using your own historical data to make the AI understand your specific industry jargon and workflows.

What happens if I exceed my API limits?

We do not hard-stop your access. You will receive an alert at 80% and 100% usage. Any overage will be billed at a standard transparent rate of $0.001 per additional query, or you can auto-upgrade your tier.
