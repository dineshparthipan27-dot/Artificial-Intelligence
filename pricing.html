Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about billing, limits, and security.

What happens if I exceed my monthly API limits? +

If you are on the Starter or Pro plan, your requests will be soft-capped. You will receive an email notification at 80% and 100% usage. To ensure uninterrupted service, you can enable "Auto-Scale" in your dashboard, which charges a nominal fee of $0.05 per 1,000 extra requests.

Is my proprietary data used to train your public models? +

Absolutely not. We have a strict zero-data-retention policy for Pro and Enterprise users. Your inputs and outputs are encrypted and isolated. We never use your proprietary data to train our foundational models.

Can I switch between monthly and annual billing? +

Yes! You can switch from monthly to annual billing at any time from your account settings. When you upgrade to annual, the 20% discount will be applied immediately, and your billing cycle will reset.

Do you offer a free trial for the Pro plan? +

Yes, we offer a 14-day fully-featured free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card is required to start testing our high-speed APIs and advanced analytics.

How long does custom model fine-tuning take? +

For Pro users, fine-tuning a custom model typically takes between 2 to 6 hours depending on your dataset size. Enterprise users get dedicated GPU clusters for near-instant training capabilities.
